The Glass

This is the real shader, and you can edit it. Change a number, press Run, watch the portal change. Break it and the compiler will tell you which line — that's not failure, that's the whole way shader people learn. Your sliders keep working as long as you keep the uniform lines at the top: a uniform is a value fed into the shader from outside — each one is wired to a slider.

Render Clip

Offline render — every frame is computed at exact time steps at full resolution, independent of your screen or GPU speed. For film work: the PNG sequence imports into DaVinci Resolve as lossless footage (drag the unzipped folder into the media pool). Keep this tab focused while rendering. Drift mode, if on, is captured deterministically.
